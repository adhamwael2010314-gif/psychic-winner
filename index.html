Overview

Four rooms. One moor. No shortcuts.

Darkmoor is a live escape room center tucked into Dokki — every room built as a self-contained world, locked, timed, and unforgiving of guesswork.

Teams are sealed into a set built for the story, not the selfie. Every lock, drawer, and floorboard is part of the puzzle. A games master watches from the fog and steps in only if the moor is about to win too easily.
