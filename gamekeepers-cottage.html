The Gamekeeper's Cottage

Smallest door, easiest entry — a good first room. A missing gamekeeper, a locked cellar, and a moor that doesn't give things back.

The Story

The estate's gamekeeper stopped filing his weekly reports three weeks ago. His cottage is exactly as he left it — boots by the door, kettle on the stove, cellar locked from a side nobody can explain. The estate wants answers before they send anyone else out to look.

Built as Darkmoor's entry room: the puzzles are fair, the searching is contained to a few rooms, and the games master steps in quickly if your group gets stuck. Still moody, still locked in — just built to be won.

What To Expect

  • Our easiest room — recommended for first-timers, families, and mixed-age groups
  • No jump scares; tension comes from the mystery, not sudden shocks
  • Straightforward puzzle logic, minimal reading required
  • Great warm-up before booking a harder room later the same night
