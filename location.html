Before You Come

A few things to know

Do I need to book in advance?

Walk-ins are welcome after 4 PM, but rooms fill up on weekends. Calling ahead is the only way to guarantee your slot and room choice.

Is Darkmoor suitable for kids?

The Gamekeeper's Cottage runs easy-to-medium and works well for families. The Asylum Ward is built to be genuinely unsettling and isn't recommended for younger players.

What if my group is smaller or larger than the room's range?

Call and tell us your number — most rooms flex by a player or two, and larger groups can often be split across two rooms running back to back.

Is there parking nearby?

Street parking is available around Gamal Salem St; it's easiest to have your driver drop you at the entrance if you're coming by car.
