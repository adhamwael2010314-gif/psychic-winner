Room 03 · Medium–Hard

Blackwood Laboratory

A chemist's workshop mid-experiment. Formulas, cages, and a machine that's still running whether you solve it or not.

The Story

Dr. Blackwood left mid-formula. The notebooks are still open, the burner's cold now but was clearly running recently, and the ventilation system is doing something it shouldn't. Your job: finish — or stop — whatever he started, before the room decides for you.

Unlike the other rooms, the clock here isn't just for show. A visible apparatus runs throughout the session, and the puzzles are built around reacting to it rather than solving in isolation. Chemistry knowledge isn't required — attention is.

What To Expect

  • Our shortest, most tightly paced room at 45 minutes
  • A live mechanical element that keeps pressure on the whole session
  • Logic and sequence puzzles more than searching — good for methodical teams
  • Popular as a second room for groups who did the Cottage first
