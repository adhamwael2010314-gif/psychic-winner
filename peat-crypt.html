Room 02 · Medium

The Peat Crypt

Sunk below the moor itself. A sealed sarcophagus and a countdown carved in a language no one alive reads fluently.

The Story

Peat cutters found the entrance by accident — a stone lintel where no stone should be, three meters under the bog. What's below wasn't built to be found, and it wasn't built to be left open, either.

Your group is the second team down. The first left most of their equipment behind. The crypt keeps its own time: the countdown carved into the far wall started the moment the door opened, and nobody's worked out yet what happens when it reaches zero.

What To Expect

  • Our biggest room — comfortably runs groups up to eight
  • Heavy on physical exploration: false walls, buried compartments, weight-triggered mechanisms
  • Atmospheric rather than jump-scare heavy — a slow, close kind of tense
  • Popular for corporate groups; splits well into two sub-teams working in parallel
