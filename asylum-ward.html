The Asylum Ward

A shuttered psychiatric ward, three patient files, and a doctor who never signed out.

The Story

Darkmoor Asylum closed in 1974, officially for "structural concerns." The paperwork stops there. Your group is let in through the staff entrance to look for a missing patient file — the one the hospital board would rather stayed missing.

The ward hasn't been cleaned since closing day. Beds are still made. A reel-to-reel recorder in the nurse's office still has tape on it. Whatever happened here, it happened fast, and the building has been quietly keeping the details ever since.

What To Expect

  • Our most difficult room — built for teams who've cleared a room or two before
  • Physical searching, lock combinations, and one genuinely unsettling jump moment
  • A games master watching throughout; hints available on request, never forced
  • Not recommended for players under 13 or anyone sensitive to strobe lighting
